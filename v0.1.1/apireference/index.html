ASEconvert.convert_ase — Method
convert_ase(system::AbstractSystem)

Convert a passed system (which satisfies the AtomsBase.AbstractSystem interface) to an ase.Atoms datastructure. Conversions to other ASE objects from equivalent Julia objects may be added in the future.
